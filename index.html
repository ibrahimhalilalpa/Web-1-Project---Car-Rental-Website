What Should I Consider When Renting a Car?

We have listed the points you need to pay attention to when renting a car on the most suitable way.

  • Compare and examine the vehicle alternatives of different rent a car companies in detail.
  • Choose from the tools that fit your needs. For example, if you have a large number of suitcases, make sure that the luggage volume of the vehicle you are renting is large.
  • Check the address of the place where you will pick up and deliver the car you rented. You can consider the alternative of delivering the vehicle at a different location than the one you received it from.
  • Some companies can deliver the car you will rent to your address. You can benefit from companies that provide this service.
  • Remember that at least 2 years must pass from the date you got your license to rent a car.
  • Make sure that you meet the necessary conditions for the vehicle you will rent.
  • Read the terms of use carefully when renting a car.
  • When taking delivery of your vehicle, carefully examine the insurance and contract, and check the vehicle's exterior and interior before taking delivery of the vehicle.
